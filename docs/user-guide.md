# TJC Stock Media User Guide

## What This Is

ResourceSpace is the front door for finding and requesting safe-to-use church media. Google Shared Drive remains the master warehouse behind the scenes.

## Search And Download

1. Open the church ResourceSpace link.
2. Search by normal words such as `Bible`, `worship`, `fellowship`, `MVP`, `baptism`, or `background`.
3. Prefer assets marked `Portal Ready` for public reuse or `Internal Ready` for ministry-only reuse.
4. Treat `Approved Public` and `Approved Internal` as ResourceSpace status, not automatic download permission.
5. Download approved use copies only when the portal shows download access.
6. If an asset is `Needs Review`, `Needs portal review`, or blocked by source/rights/people review, do not publish it.

## Upload

Upload normal photos and small files through ResourceSpace.

Every upload starts as:

```text
Needs Review / Do Not Publish
```

A reviewer must approve it before other users treat it as safe for public/internal use.

## Large Files

Large video/audio files should not be uploaded through the browser.

Use Shared Drive Incoming or give the files to the DAM admin. The file will still be reviewed and searchable in ResourceSpace after admin intake.

## What Not To Do

- Do not publish `Needs Review` assets.
- Do not upload random files directly into master folders.
- Do not rename or move master originals.
- Do not assume AI tags mean approval.
- Do not use media with children, worship, music, sermons, or unclear people/rights unless approved.

## Ask For Help

Ask the DAM admin for upload/import issues. Ask the appointed reviewer for approval and rights questions.
