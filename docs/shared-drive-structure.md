## Rule

Shared Drive is the master copy and organized storage layer. ResourceSpace is the DAM index/search/review layer.

## MVP Behavior

- Do not move or delete legacy originals.
- Copy selected MVP assets into Shared Drive when needed.
- Approved samples can be copied/exported into approved folders.
- Metadata exports go to `99_Metadata_Exports`.
